## Modulation (SET IN MATRIX)

**Go to the Matrix section on your MiniFreak to create these routings.**

| Source | Destination | Amount |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| LFO 1 (Random, slow) | Pitch | Very subtle (tape drift) |
| LFO 2 (Sine, slow) | Cutoff | Subtle |

| Slot | Effect | Settings |
|------|--------|----------|
| FX1 | **Chorus** | Rate: Slow, Depth: ~20%, Mix: ~25% |
| FX2 | **Distortion** | Drive: ~10 o'clock (tape-like) |

Detuned, warped pad that sounds like it's playing off an old VHS tape. Slow attack, warbly character.
